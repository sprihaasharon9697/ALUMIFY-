About Alumify

Registered as Society: Alumify, the Alumni Association of GP Bhubaneswar (referred to as the Association), is an independent society established to strengthen connections among graduates of Government Polytechnic Bhubaneswar (referred to as the Institute). Formally registered on 5th April 2017 under Registration No. 2589-11, Alumify operates in alignment with the Societies Registration Act, 1860.

Office: The Association’s registered office is located at Government Polytechnic Bhubaneswar, 751003. An Alumni Relations Office on Campus Block-C has been designated to manage alumni affairs, spearheaded by Prof. Rajesh Kumar, Dean of Alumni Relations. He is supported by a team of faculty coordinators to foster meaningful connections with the GP Bhubaneswar alumni community.

Mission: Alumify is committed to:

  • Fostering a vibrant network for alumni engagement and interaction;
  • Supporting alumni in achieving personal and professional goals;
  • Assisting alumni during critical times;
  • Enabling a sustained connection between alumni and their Alma Mater;
  • Enhancing the institute's prominence through collaboration in academia, research, and community outreach;
  • Operating on a ‘no profit no loss’ basis to maintain transparency;
  • Encouraging best practices across fields like engineering, technology, humanities, and social service for societal benefit;
  • Raising awareness about the institute and its alumni community;
  • Providing financial aid to deserving students from underrepresented backgrounds.

Patron: Dr. Suresh Prasad, the esteemed Director of GP Bhubaneswar, serves as the honorary Patron of Alumify. He envisions the slogan, “Strengthen the Alumni, Empower the Institute.”

Membership: The Association comprises the following membership types:

  • Regular Member: Alumni who have obtained a diploma from GP Bhubaneswar but haven’t subscribed to life membership.
  • Life Member: Alumni who have completed their diploma at GP Bhubaneswar and have paid a one-time Life Membership fee of Rs. 500.
